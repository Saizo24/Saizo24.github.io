Setting up your Workplace

Firstly, it's important to have a clean work space before you clean your PC.

Secondly, make sure to ventilate the room often, especially when you start using the air-blower. The air in the room will get dusty real quick, so keep the dust out of your lungs.

Thirdly, have proper tools. The work will get done way quicker when you have the right tools for the right job.

As you can see in the picture, I put a blanket on my table. After I'm done cleaning, all I have to do is take the blanket and shake it outside. Most of the dust will fall onto it.
