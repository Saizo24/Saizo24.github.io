PC Deep Cleaning mit Saizo

Introduction

Hello everyone, this is SaizoGaming. On this website, I'm going to show you how I'm taking my computer apart for an overdue deepcleaning session. I'm doing this before Christmas 2021, so I can start into the new Year with a more or less clean pc.

First, I'm going to take apart my pc and clean its components with cotton swabs and then remove the dust with a strong air blower.

Here are the Specs of my PCs:

  • CPU: Intel Core i7-9700f
  • RAM: 32GB DDR4 3200Mhz (4x 8GB Corsair Vengeance LPX)
  • Mainboard: Gigabyte Z390 Gaming X
  • Graphics Card: Gigabyte GeForce RTX 2070 Super Gaming OC
  • CPU-Cooling: MSI MAG CoreLiquid 240R
  • PSU: Seasonic Core GC
  • Case: MSI MAG Forge 100R
